About the Rehab Program

Our rehab program is quite extensive. We not only provide rehab to the animals and exotic birds, but we also collobarate with other veterinary and specialist. Since the rehab program was started, more than a 1,000 birds have been treated and released back into the wild. Supporters can release a raptor, adopt a bird, make a charitable donation, purchase a membership or sponsor a capital improvement project.This is also an educational center for anyone interestedin learning more about what is done heretion, purchase a membership or sponsor a capital improvement project.
