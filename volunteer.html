Volunteering

Thank you for your interest in the Animal Rescue Center (ARC)! We have many Volunteer opportunities available for all ages. High Schoolers can get service hours on adoption days. We hold fund-raising events as well as attend community events throughout the year where helping hands and talents are needed. Providing transport for animals and keeping our facility in shape among other things.After receipt and review of your application, you will be contacted by our Volunteer Coordinator. You may be asked to provide a reference for your application at that time. If you have not been contacted by ARC within 10 days of mailing (or emailing) your application, please contact us at animalcommunitycenter@acc.com or 440-942-1753.
